# Experiment: Thermal Condensation Risk

## Purpose

Validate whether thermal surface maps plus BMS humidity can estimate condensation and mold risk.

## Setup

- MLX90640 facing wall/window/wood panel
- BMS mock humidity and room temperature
- Controlled humidity changes
- Cooling or cold surface condition

## Inputs

- Thermal matrix
- Surface temperature min/avg/max
- Cold spot count
- Thermal gradient score
- Dew point margin

## Output

- condensation_risk
- mold_risk
- comfort_degradation_risk
